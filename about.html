Hi, I'm the Founder

I have been investing for about 10 years with a strong focus on dividend investing. What started as a personal passion for building reliable income portfolios quickly turned into a deep interest in dividend ETFs.

Over the years, I became frustrated with how scattered and inconsistent the available data was. I was constantly jumping between multiple websites, spreadsheets, and broker tools just to compare yields, tax treatment, expense ratios, total returns, and other important metrics.

That's why I created TopDividendETFsPro.com. My goal was to build the clean, comprehensive, and trustworthy resource I always wished existed for dividend investors like myself.

Today, I continue to actively manage my own dividend portfolio while researching and updating the data on this site. I am committed to providing high-quality, well-organized ETF information that actually helps long-term income investors.

Our Mission

At TopDividendETFsPro.com, our mission is to empower long-term dividend investors with accurate, well-organized, and actionable ETF data. We believe that with the right information, anyone can build a strong, reliable dividend portfolio that generates consistent income over time.

We focus exclusively on dividend ETFs because we believe they are one of the most effective tools for building sustainable income in a portfolio. Our aim is to be the most reliable and user-friendly destination for serious dividend ETF research.
